Privacy Policy
Last updated: 17th September 2025
We at WordJam respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

  • State information: We collect your current state (region) for basic app functionality.
  • Game Progress: Your gameplay progress (levels completed, points earned, achievements) is stored securely on Firebase so you can continue where you left off.
  • Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players use WordJam. This helps us improve the game experience. Analytics data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
  • Game Center Data: If you choose to use Apple's Game Center, Apple provides us with necessary identifiers such as match IDs to enable multiplayer matches and leaderboards.

Important: We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

How We Use Information

  • To save and sync your game progress across sessions.
  • To provide multiplayer features through Apple's Game Center.
  • To analyze gameplay trends and improve app performance and user experience.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.
  • Game progress is stored on Firebase (Google's secure cloud platform).
  • Multiplayer features rely on Apple's Game Center, which has its own privacy policy.

Security

We rely on Apple and Firebase's security measures to protect your data. While no system is 100% secure, we follow industry practices to safeguard your information.

Data Retention

We only store minimal gameplay-related data (points, levels, match IDs). No personal data is collected. Data is retained as long as you use the app.

Children's Privacy

WordJam is intended for players aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since no personal information is collected, requests such as data deletion or export are generally not applicable. However, you may manage your Game Center account through Apple directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in this section, and we encourage you to review it periodically.

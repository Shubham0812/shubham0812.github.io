Who Goes? Truth or Dare & Group Games

Privacy Policy

Effective June 11, 2026

Who Goes? is a quick finger picker for group games, icebreakers, team choices, and truth-or-dare moments. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the app.
01 No Account Required
Who Goes? does not require account creation. You do not need to provide your name, email address, phone number, or a profile to use the app.
No sign-up, no player profiles, and no contact list access.
02 Finger Touches Stay on Your Device
The app detects touches on the screen so it can choose a player, build teams, order players, or run other local game modes. Touch positions, finger counts, and game results are processed on your device for the current round and are not sent to our servers.
Finger picker rounds Team assignments Elimination rounds Truth or Dare choices
03 Data Stored Locally
Who Goes? stores app preferences on your device, such as sound and haptic settings, selected game mode, selected Truth or Dare pack, appearance choices, Plus unlock status cache, and limited usage counters used for feature access.
App settings Selected modes and packs Theme preferences Daily play counters
04 iCloud Sync
Who Goes? may use Apple's iCloud and CloudKit services to sync a small rating-prompt state, such as when you last tapped to rate the app, so the same prompt is not repeatedly shown across your devices. This data is handled by Apple under your iCloud account settings.
05 In-App Purchases
Who Goes? offers an optional lifetime Plus purchase. Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details.

RevenueCat is used to validate purchases and manage access to Plus features. RevenueCat may use an anonymous app user identifier, purchase receipts, product identifiers, entitlement status, and related transaction information needed to confirm access.
06 Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking
Who Goes? does not sell personal information and does not use third-party advertising frameworks. The app does not track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising purposes, and it does not request App Tracking Transparency permission.
No ads, no cross-app tracking, and no contact harvesting.
07 Data Sharing
Limited technical and purchase-related data may be processed by service providers that make the app work:
Apple
App Store purchases, refunds, iCloud, diagnostics
RevenueCat
Purchase validation and Plus access

We do not sell this information. Service providers process data according to their own privacy policies and only for the services they provide.

08 Children's Privacy
Who Goes? is a general-audience group game utility and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
09 Data Retention
Local app preferences remain on your device until you change them or delete the app. iCloud data can be managed through your Apple ID and iCloud settings. Purchase records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat as needed to provide purchase access, restore purchases, comply with law, and prevent fraud.

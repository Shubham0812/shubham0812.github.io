Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026

SF Catalog is a developer tool designed to help iOS developers explore and work with SF Symbols. Your privacy is important. This policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. No Account Required

SF Catalog does not require account creation. You are not required to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Bookmarks
  • Custom collections
  • App preferences and settings

If iCloud sync is enabled on your device, this data may be synced securely using Apple’s iCloud services.

3. In-App Purchases

SF Catalog offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple.

RevenueCat is used to validate and manage purchase status. RevenueCat may generate an anonymous identifier to manage subscriptions and purchase access. No personal information is attached by the app.

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

SF Catalog uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand feature usage and improve stability.

  • Analytics data may include app interactions, device model, operating system version, and general usage patterns.
  • Crash reports may include technical diagnostic information to help fix issues.

This information is used solely to improve the app’s performance and functionality. The app does not use analytics for advertising or tracking across other apps.

5. No Advertising or Tracking

SF Catalog does not use advertising frameworks. The app does not track users across third-party apps or websites. App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission is not requested.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited technical data may be processed by:

  • Apple (for in-app purchases and iCloud sync)
  • Google Firebase (for analytics and crash reporting)
  • RevenueCat (for purchase validation)

7. Children’s Privacy

SF Catalog is intended for developers and is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.

8. Data Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you delete the app. Analytics and diagnostic data retention is governed by the respective service providers.

9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as features evolve. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date.

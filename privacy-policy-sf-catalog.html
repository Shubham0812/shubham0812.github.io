SF Catalog iOS Developer Tool

Privacy Policy

Effective February 22, 2026

SF Catalog is a developer tool designed to help iOS developers explore and work with SF Symbols. Your privacy matters. This policy explains clearly how information is handled when you use the app — written to be read, not buried.
01 No Account Required
SF Catalog does not require account creation. You are not required to provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number to use the app.
No sign-up. No email required. Just open the app and start exploring.
02 Data Stored on Your Device
The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:
Bookmarks Custom collections App preferences & settings

If iCloud sync is enabled on your device, this data may be synced securely using Apple's iCloud services.

03 In-App Purchases
SF Catalog offers optional in-app purchases processed by Apple through the App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple.

RevenueCat is used to validate and manage purchase status. RevenueCat may generate an anonymous identifier to manage subscriptions and purchase access. No personal information is attached by the app.
04 Analytics & Crash Reporting
SF Catalog uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand feature usage and improve stability.
App interactions Device model OS version Usage patterns Crash diagnostics

This information is used solely to improve performance and functionality. The app does not use analytics for advertising or tracking across other apps.

05 No Advertising or Tracking
SF Catalog does not use advertising frameworks. The app does not track users across third-party apps or websites. App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission is not requested.
Zero ad frameworks. Zero cross-app tracking. Your focus stays on your work.
06 Data Sharing
We do not sell personal information. Limited technical data may be processed by the following service providers:
Apple
Purchases & iCloud sync
Google Firebase
Analytics & crash reporting
RevenueCat
Purchase validation
07 Children's Privacy
SF Catalog is intended for developers and is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.
08 Data Retention
Local app data remains on your device until you delete the app. Analytics and diagnostic data retention is governed by the respective service providers (Google Firebase, RevenueCat).
09 Changes to This Policy
This policy may be updated as features evolve. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date shown at the top of the page.
